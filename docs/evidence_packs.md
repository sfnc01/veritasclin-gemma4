# Evidence Packs

An Evidence Pack is a portable record of an evidence review.

It includes:

- Metadata and topic.
- Safety context.
- PICO extraction.
- PubMed search strategy.
- Ranked evidence.
- Claim Ledger.
- Caution and conflict map.
- Evidence freshness.
- Executive and patient-friendly summaries.
- JSON, Markdown, CSV, and caution-map exports.

## Portability

The pack can be moved to a low-connectivity setting and loaded offline. Offline Q&A uses only the loaded pack.

## Reproducibility

The PubMed query, search time, source records, claims, and support status are preserved for audit.

The pack also preserves freshness metadata and caution items so a field team can see
whether the evidence is current, indirect, low certainty, mismatched, or insufficient.
